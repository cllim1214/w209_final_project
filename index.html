Home

The goal of this project is to educate cryptocurrency and blockchain enthusiast about the industry without looking into financial data. We want to look at a chain's performance indicators, such as developer activity and total value locked (TVL). Developer activity is important for understanding where talent and investments are focused. TVL is also important because it is the amount of liquidity and assets present on each chain. It is one of the main demonstrations of active use and value being generated by the chain.

About

A blockchain is a type of decentralized database shared across a peer-to-peer network. Unlike a typical database where data is structured into tables, a blockchain is structured into data blocks. When a block reaches its storage capacity, it is closed and linked to the previous block via cryptography, forming a blockchain. An important thing to note is that decentralized blockchains are immutable. The data is secure and cannot be altered, deleted, or destroyed. As a result of these benefits, we are beginning to see the application of blockchain technology across various sectors.
